8-Day Workout Plan

This is my 8-day workout plan

Program Overview

Duration

8 days of progressive training

Frequency

4-5 days per week

Focus

Muscular Endurance, Cardiovascular Endurance & Muscular Strength

Goal

Build muscle & increase endurance

8-Day Cycle

  • Day 1: Upper Body Strength
  • Day 2: Lower Body Power
  • Day 3: Rest or Light Cardio
  • Day 4: Upper Body — Muscle Growth
  • Day 5: Lower Body — Muscle Growth
  • Day 6: Full Body Conditioning
  • Day 7: Active Recovery
  • Day 8: Power & Explosiveness

Day 1: Upper Body Strength

Upper Body Strength Session

4 sets x 6 reps | Rest: 2 min

4 sets x 6 reps | Rest: 2 min

Day 3: Rest or Light Cardio

Recovery day - focus on active recovery and mobility work.

Active Recovery Protocol

  • 20-30 minutes of light cardio (walking, cycling, swimming)
  • 10-15 minutes of stretching and mobility work
  • Foam rolling for 5-10 minutes
  • Yoga or pilates (optional)
  • Focus on relaxation and recovery

Day 7: Active Recovery

Recovery day - focus on mobility and light movement.

Recovery Protocol

  • 15-20 minutes of light walking or yoga
  • Comprehensive stretching routine (20 minutes)
  • Foam rolling and myofascial release (10-15 minutes)
  • Meditation or breathwork (5-10 minutes)
  • Avoid intense training on this day
  • Focus on mental and physical recovery

Important Tips for Success

Nutrition

Eat in a slight surplus (300-500 calories) with adequate protein (1g per pound of bodyweight)

Sleep

Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep per night for optimal recovery

Hydration

Drink plenty of water throughout the day, especially before, during, and after workouts

Tracking

Keep a workout log to track weights, reps, and progress over time

Consistency

Stick to the program for all 8 days to see optimal results

Safety

Warm up properly and use good form. Consider working with a trainer if new to lifting
